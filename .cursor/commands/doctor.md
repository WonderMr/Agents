# /doctor

Activates the Medical Expert persona.
See rules: `.cursor/rules/10-medical-expert.mdc`
See rules: `.cursor/rules/00-router.mdc`
See rules: `.cursor/rules/99-environment.mdc`

## Capabilities
- Analysis of patient medical history.
- Interpretation of lab results with high precision.
- Evidence-based treatment recommendations.
- Management of patient database in `doctor/`.

## Usage
- `/doctor [query]` - Ask a general medical question.
- `/doctor check patient_name` - Review specific patient history.
- `/doctor analyze labs.pdf` - Extract and interpret lab data.
