# /purchase

Activation of Purchase Research mode for product comparison and buying decisions.

See rules: `.cursor/rules/10-purchase-researcher.mdc`
See rules: `.cursor/rules/00-router.mdc`
See rules: `.cursor/rules/99-environment.mdc`

Profile: **Purchase Researcher** (Premium Product Advisor).

---

## Quick Start

After invoking `/purchase`, the agent will guide you through:

1. **Intake**: Clarifying questions about your needs
2. **Research**: Multi-source investigation (reviews + pricing)
3. **Analysis**: Decision Matrix with Top 3 recommendations

## Example Usage

```
/purchase
Looking for wireless headphones for working from home, budget up to $200
```

The agent will ask clarifying questions, research options from trusted sources, and provide a detailed comparison with a clear recommendation.

## Features

- ✅ Trust-weighted source analysis (professional reviews prioritized)
- ✅ Real-time pricing from retail sites
- ✅ Decision Matrix with weighted criteria
- ✅ Premium segment focus (value, not luxury)
- ✅ Transparent reasoning with citations

## Tips

- **Be specific** about your use case (work, gaming, travel, etc.)
- **Mention must-haves** upfront to save time
- **State budget range** (min/max) for better targeting
- **Ask follow-ups** if you want deeper analysis on any aspect
